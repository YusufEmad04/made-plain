# Builder's Kit Content Plan

This folder translates the product strategy in [../the-complete-plan.md](../the-complete-plan.md), the brand strategy in [../the-complete-brand.md](../the-complete-brand.md), and the business-development knowledge base in [../business-material/business-development/README.md](../business-material/business-development/README.md) into an operating content plan.

The plan is split into four files:

- [01-content-strategy-foundation.md](./01-content-strategy-foundation.md) - core value, content role, pillars, rules, and format system.
- [02-30-day-content-plan-may-2026.md](./02-30-day-content-plan-may-2026.md) - detailed daily plan for May 1-30, 2026.
- [03-90-day-content-plan.md](./03-90-day-content-plan.md) - higher-level May 1-July 29, 2026 content arc.
- [04-content-plan-critique.md](./04-content-plan-critique.md) - third-person critique of the plan, including strengths, risks, and recommended improvements.

The guiding idea is simple: the content is the public proof of the kit. Every post should make one business concept easier to understand, easier to connect, and easier to use.
